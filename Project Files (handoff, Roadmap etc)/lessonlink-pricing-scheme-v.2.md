**Format options:**

- Cover the same curriculum

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What if a student can't commit to 60 hours upfront?**
A: Start with the 10-pack (в‚¬285.10) to build trust. Most students upgrade after seeing progress.

**Q: Can students pause mid-course?**
A: Yes, courses can be paused for up to 12 months while maintaining the discount rate.

**Q: What if a student wants to switch between 30-min and 60-min lessons?**
A: Both formats work toward the same 60-hour total. Students can mix if desired (e.g., 30 x 60-min + 60 x 30-min).

**Q: Are Full Courses refundable?**
A: Partial refunds available for unused lessons (minus processing fees). Terms specified in purchase agreement.

**Q: Do certificates have value?**
A: Yes - certificates show completed CEFR levels and are recognized by schools/employers as proof of study hours.

**Q: How long does a Full Course take?**
A: Depends on frequency:
- 2 lessons/week = 30 weeks (7.5 months)
- 3 lessons/week = 20 weeks (5 months)
- 4 lessons/week = 15 weeks (3.75 months)
